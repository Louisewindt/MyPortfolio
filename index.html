About me

Hello! I'm Louise. A 23-year-old multimedia designer with a never-ending love for design systems, a strong flair for UI/UX, and just the right amount of obsession with Figma and Photoshop. Based in Sønderborg — and always ready with a sticky note or ten to solve a problem.

Although my creative spark started way before, I kicked things off properly when I began the Multimedia Design program at EASV in 2023, graduating in 2025.

My skills include but are not limited to: UI Design, UX Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, HTML/CSS, Content Creation, and branding that doesn’t make your eyes hurt.

Oh, and did I mention? I’m quick to learn, full of ideas, and extremely allergic to confusing interfaces.
